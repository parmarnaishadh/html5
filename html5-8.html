Authentic & Flavorful Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 Pav (bread rolls)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes, capsicum, green peas, and cook until soft.
  4. Mix in mashed potatoes, pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, and salt.
  5. Mash everything together while cooking for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Toast pav with butter on a pan until golden brown.
  7. Garnish the bhaji with coriander leaves and serve hot with lemon wedges.

Authentic Mumbai-Style Vada Pav Recipe ‚Äì Spicy, Crispy & Irresistible!

Ingredients:

For Vada (Potato Filling):

  • 3 medium potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ¬Ω tsp turmeric powder
  • ¬Ω tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

For Batter:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • ¬Ω tsp turmeric powder
  • ¬Ω tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil (for deep frying)

For Assembly:

  • 4 pav (soft bread rolls)
  • 2 tbsp garlic chutney
  • 2 green chilies (fried, optional)
  • Butter (for toasting pav)

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Potato Filling

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, and turmeric powder.
  3. Add mashed potatoes, salt, lemon juice, and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  4. Let it cool, then make small round balls.

Step 2: Make the Batter & Fry

  1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to make a thick, smooth batter.
  3. Heat oil for deep frying.
  4. Dip each potato ball into the batter and deep-fry until golden brown.

Step 3: Assemble the Vada Pav

  1. Slice the pav buns and toast them with butter.
  2. Spread garlic chutney inside the pav.
  3. Place a hot vada inside and press gently.
  4. Serve with fried green chilies and enjoy!
